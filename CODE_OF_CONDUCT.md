# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making MiraOS a welcoming, respectful, and constructive project for everyone. Participation in this community should be free from harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- being kind and respectful in discussions
- giving and receiving feedback constructively
- assuming good intent and communicating clearly
- helping others learn and contribute

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, insults, or threats
- discriminatory or exclusionary language
- sharing private information without permission
- disruptive or destructive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or serious violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from participation.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces and public channels where MiraOS is discussed or represented.
